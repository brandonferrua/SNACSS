SNACSS comes with a calculated typographical scale that's based off of your top level heirarchical heading element, the h1. This value can be changed by altering the $heading-fontSize variable within your project _vars.scss file. As a default, this value is set to 68px which calculates into 4.25em to provide a relative scale based on the variable change.

Body

SNACSS also comes pre-calculated with the typographical scale for body copy. Within your _vars.scss file, you can adjust the $font-size variable to alter the veritcal rhythm of your site.
